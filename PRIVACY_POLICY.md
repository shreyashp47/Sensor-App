# Privacy Policy

**Shreyash Pattewar** built **SensorApp** as a free app. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

## Information Collection and Use

SensorApp does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data, usage data, or device identifiers to any external servers, third parties, or the developer.

### Sensor Data

- All sensor readings (accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer, etc.) are processed **locally on your device**.
- Logged sensor data is stored **only in the app's local Room database** on your device.
- **No sensor data is ever sent over the network** or shared with any third party.
- You can export sensor data as a CSV file to your Downloads folder — this stays on your device unless you manually share it.

### Permissions

- **ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION**: Used only when you tap the Step Counter sensor, to read step count data. This data never leaves your device.
- **BODY_SENSORS**: Used only when you tap supported health sensors. Data stays on your device.

### Third-Party Services

SensorApp does **not** use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

## Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by:

1. Opening the app's **Settings** screen and clearing database data, or
2. Going to **Android Settings → Apps → SensorApp → Storage → Clear data**, or
3. Uninstalling the app.
